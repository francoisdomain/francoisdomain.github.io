Biography

François is a writer known for exploring identity, blurred lines between sexuality stereotypes, and incorporating dark humor in his fiction. His stories delve deep into the complexities of the human experience with a subtle but impactful narrative style.

Latest Novel: "Above the Waterline"

"Above the Waterline" is François' latest novel, which challenges perceptions of love, loss, and the search for identity.
