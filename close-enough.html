François Domain is a French-English writer based in London, known for his introspective works that explore the fragility of human relationships and the inner turmoil of love and identity. Blending psychological thriller and detective fiction, Domain’s narratives center on complex characters navigating fluid boundaries of intimacy and connection. His methodical approach, drawn from his career as a technical architect, results in intricately woven stories that challenge traditional views on love and relationships.
